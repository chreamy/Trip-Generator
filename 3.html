Exploring Monuments: A Journey of History and Culture
Welcome to our Monuments-themed trip to Boston! On this trip, you'll get to explore some of the most iconic monuments in the city. First, you'll visit the Bunker Hill Monument, a 221-foot granite obelisk that commemorates the Battle of Bunker Hill. You'll be able to climb the 294 steps to the top of the monument and take in the breathtaking views of the city. Next, you'll visit the 1767 Milestones, a series of granite markers that were placed along the roads of colonial Boston to mark the distance to the city center. You'll also get to explore the Lucius Clapp Memorial, a bronze statue of a Revolutionary War soldier that stands in the center of Boston Common. This trip is sure to be an unforgettable experience, so don't miss out!
